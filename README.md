## Understanding DA and PA

Before we delve into the strategies, it’s essential to understand what DA and PA are. Domain Authority is a metric developed by Moz that predicts how well a website will rank on search engine result pages (SERPs). It ranges from 1 to 100, with higher scores indicating a greater ability to rank. Page Authority, on the other hand, measures the ranking strength of individual pages rather than entire domains.

High DA PA backlinks are like votes of confidence from authoritative websites. They signal to search engines that your content is valuable and trustworthy.
